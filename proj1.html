Wage Gap Dashboard

This dashboard compares the median wage for a given year to other groups in that same year. Furthermore, graphs of change over the years, sorted by ethnicity, gender, and education, are provided to observe how wages have changed throughout the years. Overall, white individuals, men, and college graduates tend to have wages that are above the median, while all other groups are below the median.
